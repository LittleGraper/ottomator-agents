GeoEnrichment service enhances your geographic data with localized information about people, places, and businesses. By submitting a point, polygon, address, or place name the service can tell you about the habits and lifestyles of those who live there, the types of nearby businesses, the postal codes in the area, and so on.

The following table lists the countries or areas for which there is GeoEnrichment data. 

<Note type={"note"}>

<br />
-   Countries marked with an asterisk \* have incomplete coverage due to trans-disputed areas or regions that are sparsely populated.
-   Countries marked with a diesis ‡ don't contain all 5 variables in the [KeyGlobalFacts](/geoenrichment-coverage#keyglobalfacts-variables) data collection. 

</Note>

<Note type={"note"}>

Australia data collections, provided by MapData Services, was deprecated in release 7.3.

</Note>
