The sections below outline basic limitations to take into consideration when working with the ArcGIS Online and ArcGIS Enterprise Sharing API. This is a dynamic list that may be updated as new functionality is introduced.

## Search limitations

<StyledTable headers={"Limitation type,Maximum limit"}>
  <StyledTableRow>
    <StyledTableCell cellStyle="markdown">

      Maximum [Search results](/search) per request or page

    </StyledTableCell>
    <StyledTableCell cellStyle="markdown">

      Maximum of 100 results returned.

    </StyledTableCell>
  </StyledTableRow>
  <StyledTableRow>
    <StyledTableCell cellStyle="markdown">

    </StyledTableCell>

    <StyledTableCell cellStyle="markdown">

    </StyledTableCell>
  </StyledTableRow>
</StyledTable>

  </StyledTableRow>
  <StyledTableRow>

  </StyledTableRow>
  <StyledTableRow>
    <StyledTableCell cellStyle="markdown">

      100

    </StyledTableCell>

  <StyledTableRow>
    <StyledTableCell cellStyle="markdown">
